Badminton is a racquet sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net. Although it may be played with the most common forms of the game are "singles" (with one player per side) and "doubles" (with two players per side). often played as a casual outdoor activity in a yard or on a beach; formal games are played on a rectangular indoor court scored by striking the shuttlecock with the racquet and landing it within the opposing side's half of the court. Each side may only strike the shuttlecock once before it passes over the net. Play ends once the shuttlecock has struck if a fault has been called by the umpire, service judge, or (in their absence) the opposing side.
